Skip re-emission new
When Telemetry.log mirrors to Rails.logger, the bridge must NOT re-emit to OTel. Thread-local guard prevents double emission.
Deduplication is critical. Without it, Telemetry.log(:info, msg) would emit the same log record twice — once via emit_otel directly, and once when the mirrored Rails.logger.info(msg) triggers the bridge. A thread-local flag (Thread.current[:telemetry_skip_otel_bridge]) prevents re-entrant emission.
